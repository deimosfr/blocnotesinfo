Introduction

This site is dedicated to my memory that often fail me. Since it is more interesting to work on new projects that seek what has been studied, I create here a notepad allowing me to remember and share my knowledge. When I work on new projects, I search and read the documentation and then write my own which helps me to remember things. There are currently about 379 documentation since 05/08/2006.
For my work, I also have to do it in French, this is why some articles are in French and unfortunately not translated.

This wiki is static verion, no major changes will occur. If you want to continue to follow my technical topics, please look at https://blog.deimos.fr.
